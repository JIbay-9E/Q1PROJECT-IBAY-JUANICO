About our chairs

Founded in 1965, our company began as a modest workshop in a small town, driven by a passion for quality craftsmanship and the art of leatherwork. The founder, a skilled artisan, recognized the potential of leather as a material that could combine beauty, durability, and comfort. With a vision to create timeless pieces, he meticulously crafted each chair by hand, ensuring attention to detail that set our products apart. As word spread about our exceptional chairs, demand grew, allowing us to expand our operations while maintaining our commitment to quality. Throughout the 1970s and 1980s, we introduced innovative designs that embraced both classic and contemporary styles, appealing to a diverse clientele. Our chairs became synonymous with elegance and comfort, gracing homes and offices across the nation. In the 1990s, we embraced new technologies while staying true to our artisanal roots, implementing sustainable practices to ensure our leather sources were environmentally friendly. This dedication to sustainability resonated with our customers, further solidifying our reputation as a responsible brand. Today, we are proud to be a leading name in the leather furniture industry, with a legacy built on craftsmanship, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. Our diverse collection of leather chairs continues to reflect our rich history while adapting to modern tastes, ensuring that each piece not only enhances your home but also tells a story of quality and tradition. As we look to the future, we remain dedicated to our founding principles, crafting chairs that invite comfort and style into every space.
